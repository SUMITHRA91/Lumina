# 🌟 Lumina – AI-Powered Mental Health Companion

## 📖 Overview

Lumina is an AI-powered mental wellness platform that provides real-time emotional support through facial emotion recognition, empathetic AI conversations, personalized therapy recommendations, and emotional analytics.

The platform acts as a compassionate digital companion that continuously understands a user's emotional state and offers personalized wellness guidance, therapeutic exercises, and crisis support when needed.

Unlike traditional mental health applications that rely solely on user input, Lumina combines computer vision, conversational AI, emotional intelligence, and wellness planning to create a proactive and personalized mental health experience.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Mental health issues such as stress, anxiety, loneliness, burnout, and emotional distress often go unnoticed until they become severe.

Many individuals:

* Do not have immediate access to professional support.
* Struggle to understand their emotional patterns.
* Lack personalized wellness guidance.
* Feel uncomfortable discussing mental health concerns.
* Need real-time emotional assistance during difficult moments.

Lumina addresses these challenges by providing an AI-powered companion capable of understanding emotions and delivering timely support.

---

# 🚀 Key Features

## 🎭 Real-Time Emotion Detection

Lumina analyzes facial expressions using the device camera to detect emotional states in real time.

Supported emotions include:

* Happy 😊
* Sad 😔
* Angry 😠
* Fearful 😨
* Surprised 😲
* Calm 😌

### Benefits

* Continuous emotional awareness
* Passive emotion monitoring
* Instant emotional insights

---

## 🤖 AI Mental Health Companion

Lumina includes an empathetic AI assistant capable of:

* Understanding user emotions
* Conducting supportive conversations
* Providing encouragement
* Offering coping strategies
* Delivering mental wellness suggestions

The AI adapts its responses according to detected emotional states and conversation context.

---

## 💬 Emotion-Aware Conversations

Unlike traditional chatbots, Lumina incorporates emotional signals into conversations.

Features include:

* Context-aware responses
* Emotion-sensitive dialogue
* Supportive mental wellness coaching
* Personalized interaction experiences

---

## 📊 Emotional Analytics Dashboard

Lumina tracks emotional patterns over time and generates visual insights.

Analytics include:

* Daily mood trends
* Weekly emotional summaries
* Emotional distribution charts
* Trigger identification
* Behavioral pattern recognition

This helps users better understand their emotional health journey.

---

## 🧘 Personalized Therapy Plans

Based on emotional analysis, Lumina generates customized wellness plans.

Recommendations may include:

### Physical Wellness

* Exercise plans
* Stretching routines
* Posture improvement

### Mental Wellness

* Guided breathing exercises
* Mindfulness practices
* Stress reduction activities

### Lifestyle Wellness

* Sleep recommendations
* Nutrition suggestions
* Productivity strategies

---

## 🌬 Guided Breathing Exercises

Interactive breathing sessions help users:

* Reduce stress
* Manage anxiety
* Improve focus
* Regulate emotions

Breathing exercises are personalized based on the user's current emotional state.

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Trusted Contacts System

Users can add trusted family members or friends who can be notified during emotional crises.

Features:

* Emergency contact management
* Secure contact storage
* Crisis support network
* Safety-focused design

---

## 📈 Longitudinal Emotional Tracking

Lumina continuously tracks emotional progress and generates historical insights.

This enables users to:

* Identify recurring emotional triggers
* Monitor recovery progress
* Measure wellness improvements
* Build healthier habits

---

## 🔒 Privacy-First Architecture

User privacy is a core design principle.

### Privacy Features

* Camera processing occurs locally
* No video recordings stored
* Secure authentication
* Encrypted communication
* User-controlled data access
